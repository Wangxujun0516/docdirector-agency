# User Empathy Mapping Skill

## Instructions
Define the target audience for a document and tailor content to their needs.

### Process
1. **Identify persona**: Who is reading this? (junior dev, senior engineer, ops, product manager)
2. **Assess context**: What are they trying to do? What's their emotional state? (blocked, learning, confirming)
3. **Map needs**: What information do they need first? What can wait? What must be called out?

### Guidelines
- Start with what the reader needs to accomplish, not what the system does.
- Anticipate failure modes: what's the first thing that will go wrong? Document it.
- Use language that matches the reader's familiarity level.
- When in doubt, prefer too much explanation over too little.

### Output
A brief audience analysis to accompany the document outline or draft.
